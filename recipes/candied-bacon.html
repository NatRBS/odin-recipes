Candied Bacon

Candy + Bacon

What else needs to be said?

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons maples syrup
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound thicccccc-cut bacon

Steps

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  1. Mix brown sugar, rice vinegar, maple syrup, and black pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Place bacon slices on a cooling rack set over a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, turn slices, and bake another 5 minutes.
  4. Remove bacon and brush both sides with brown sugar mixture. Return bacon to the oven and bake another 5 minutes. Repeat basting every 5 minutes until bacon is browned and crisp, about 35 minutes.
