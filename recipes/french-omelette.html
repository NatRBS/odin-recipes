French Omelette

Name a more classic pairing in the english vocabulary. The French omelette is one of France's proudest exports and one of the culinary world's most delicious gifts.

Ingredients

  • 3 large fresh eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon cold water
  • 1/4 kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 pinch cayenne or white pepper to taste
