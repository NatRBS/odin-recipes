Buttermilk Pancakes

Delicious Buttermilk Pancakes

These are some of the tastiest, fluffy, most delicious melt in your mouth pancakes in the world. And you're going to learn how to make them.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk (star ingredient)
  • 1/2 milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
